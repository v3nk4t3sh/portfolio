MUSIC FILES
===========

Drop your MP3 files in this folder with these EXACT filenames
(the music player in the hero references them directly):

  jazz-1.mp3
  jazz-2.mp3
  jazz-3.mp3

Notes:
- Track 2 (jazz-2.mp3) starts playback 4 seconds in (offset: 4).
- Track 3 (jazz-3.mp3) plays at reduced volume (0.45) — pick a louder track.
- Suggested source: Kevin MacLeod's royalty-free jazz at incompetech.com
  (CC BY 4.0 — attribution is already in the site footer).
- To change titles/artists/volumes, edit the TRACKS array in
  src/components/MusicPlayer.jsx.
